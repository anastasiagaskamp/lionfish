Lionfish
by anastasia

About

The Lionfish is a brightly colored sea animal, originating from the Indo-pacific region. This fish is poisonous, and the bright colors are warning symbols for other animals. They may also be agressive, as they have shown this behavior towards human researchers.

Diet

  • Small fish
  • Mollusks

Fun facts

  1. Scientific Name: Pterois volitans
  2. Over ten different species.
